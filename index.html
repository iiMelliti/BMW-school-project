BMW M3

The Ultimate Driving Machine

THE LEGEND

Born on the Track

The BMW M3 is a high-performance version of the BMW 3 Series, developed by BMW's in-house motorsport division, BMW M GmbH.


Since its launch in 1986, the M3 has been the benchmark for sports sedans, combining track-ready performance with everyday luxury. The current generation features aggressive styling and the legendary S58 engine.
